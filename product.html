Itinerary & Points of Interest
1
Colosseum (~1 hour 15 minutes)

Enter one of the most iconic landmarks of the ancient world. Walk through the first and second levels, explore panoramic terraces, and visit the Colosseum Museum. Learn about gladiator games, Roman engineering, and the cultural life of the empire.

2
Arch of Constantine (~10 minutes)

Admire this triumphal arch celebrating Constantine’s victory, located just outside the Colosseum.

3
Roman Forum (~1 hour)

Explore the heart of ancient Roman public life. Key highlights include:

  • Temple of Saturn
  • Arch of Septimius Severus
  • Basilica of Maxentius
  • Rostra (speaking platform)
  • Curia (Senate House)

4
Temple of Venus and Rome (~10–15 minutes)

Once the largest temple in ancient Rome, this structure honored the goddess of love and the eternal city.

5
Temple of the Dioscuri (~10–15 minutes)

Dedicated to the mythological twins Castor and Pollux — symbols of strength and protection.

3
Palatine Hill (~1 hour)

Home to Roman emperors and the legendary founding spot of Rome. Highlights include:

  • Farnese Gardens
  • Domus Augustana (Flavian Palace)
  • House of Livia
  • Panoramic views over Circus Maximus
  • Base of the Statue of Nero

Optional Stops (Time Permitting):

  • Ongoing exhibitions (included in your ticket)
